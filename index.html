Adversarial Ops is a volunteer-led nonprofit. We support victims of cyber fraud, assist with incident triage, responsible takedown requests, and education—always legal, always human-first.

We never hack back. We coordinate lawful responses, work with platforms/ISPs/LE where appropriate, and protect your privacy.

Our mission

Make practical cybersecurity help accessible to anyone—regardless of background or ability to pay. We mobilize trained volunteers (“Operators”) to provide clear guidance, triage incidents, and reduce harm.

  • Rapid, trauma‑aware support for victims of scams, account takeovers, doxxing, and data loss.
  • Lawful disruption: abuse reports, takedown requests, and coordination with providers & CERTs.
  • Education: practical guides, workshops, and 1:1 hygiene tune‑ups.

Ethical & legal

No hack‑back. We follow the law and industry best practices.

Privacy‑first

Minimal data collection. We only keep what we must.

Open & transparent

Public impact reports and budgets.

Inclusive

Help for anyone, anywhere, within scope.

What we do

Incident triage

Account takeovers, malware, fraud, doxxing. We guide mitigation, evidence capture, and safe recovery steps.

Lawful takedown coordination

We file abuse reports, work with platforms/hosts/registrars, and escalate to CERTs/LE where appropriate.

Education & prevention

Workshops, checklists, and 1:1 hardening to reduce future risk.

Governance & transparency

Adversarial Ops is structured as a nonprofit. We will publish an annual impact report, budget, and board roster. Conflicts of interest are disclosed.

  • Code of Conduct covering respect, privacy, and neutrality.
  • Intake & case‑handling SOPs with audit trails.
  • Data retention policy with minimal collection and deletion by default.
Do you hack back or take down sites yourselves?

No. We never conduct illegal activity. We coordinate lawful reports and work through proper channels.

Is it really free?

Yes. We are volunteer‑run. If you’re able, you can donate to help us scale operations (coming soon).

How do you protect my data?

We only collect necessary details to assist you, store them securely, and delete them when no longer needed.
